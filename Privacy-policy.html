Privacy Policy for PolicyHub

Effective date: April 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PolicyHub ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the PolicyHub mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Enter

  • Agent profile information (such as name, phone, email)
  • Customer and quotation details entered by you
  • Premium calculation inputs and quotation history

b) Device Permissions

  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS / WRITE_CONTACTS): Used only to help you pick or save customer details for quotations.
  • Internet / Network: Used for app functionality and ad services.

c) Advertising Data

PolicyHub uses Google Mobile Ads. Ad providers may collect certain device and usage data (for example advertising identifiers) as governed by their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Information

  • To calculate insurance premiums and generate quotations
  • To save and manage customers and quotation history
  • To improve app reliability and user experience
  • To display ads (where applicable)

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Most app data is stored locally on your device (for example customer and quotation data).
  • If you uninstall the app, locally stored data may be deleted from your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Data may be shared only in the following situations:

  • When you explicitly share quotations using your device share options
  • With third-party service providers required for ads and app operations
  • When required by applicable law or legal process

5. Third-Party Services

PolicyHub may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)
  • Platform components provided by Google Play Services

These services may process data under their own privacy terms. Please review their privacy policies for details.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data handled by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

PolicyHub is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

8. Your Choices

  • You can choose whether to grant contacts permission.
  • You can delete customer/quotation data from within the app.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Effective date" above when changes are made.
